# ArtSpark — How To Use

## Make a Lesson Plan

1. Open **artspark-alpha.vercel.app** on your phone
2. Tap **Take Photo** or **Choose from Library** to pick a photo of an art project you like
3. OR paste a link from **Instagram** or **Pinterest** into the URL box
4. Add any notes if you want (like "use only supplies we already have" or "make it easier for younger kids")
5. Tap **Generate** and wait about 15 seconds
6. Your full lesson plan appears — scroll through to see everything

## Save a Plan

1. After a plan is generated, tap **Save**
2. Find all your saved plans by tapping **Library** in the top bar

## Share on Instagram

1. Open a saved plan from your Library
2. Tap **Copy for Instagram** — this copies a ready-to-post caption with hashtags
3. Tap **Carousel Preview** to see your plan as Instagram slides
4. Tap **Download All** to save the slide images to your phone
5. Open Instagram → New Post → select the downloaded images → paste the caption

## Pin to Pinterest

1. Open a saved plan from your Library
2. Tap the red **Pin** button
3. Pinterest opens with a preview image and description ready to go

## Print a Plan

1. Open a saved plan from your Library
2. Tap **Print** — it formats nicely for paper

## Set Up Your Classroom Supplies

1. Tap the **gear icon** (top right, next to Library)
2. Check off supplies your classroom already has
3. Set your **class size** with the slider
4. Tap **Save My Supplies**
5. Now every plan will be customized — it won't tell you to buy things you already have, and quantities will match your class size

## Get Personalized Recommendations

After you've saved a few plans, the home page will show a **"For You"** section with 3 plan ideas based on what you like. Just tap one to generate the full plan — no photo needed!

## Browse All Plans

Tap **Gallery** in the top bar to see all your plans in one place. Share any plan link and it shows a nice preview with the plan title and details.
